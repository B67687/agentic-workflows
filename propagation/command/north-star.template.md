---
description: Preserve the big goal while keeping execution slice-sized
---

Use this for long-horizon goals that should stay ambitious without turning into one giant execution plan.

Pass the plain goal on the same line, like:
`/north-star recreate Elemental Battlegrounds faithfully enough to preserve the nostalgia and play feel`

First run:
`bash ./north-star.sh "$ARGUMENTS"`

Then return a compact north-star note with:
- the big user-facing goal
- what must feel faithful
- what success would prove
- what should explicitly not be solved all at once
- the next command to use
