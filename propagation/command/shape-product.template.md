---
description: Grill and compress the intended product experience before milestone planning
---

Use this when the final product experience is still too broad, emotional, nostalgic, or underspecified to plan safely.

Pass the plain goal on the same line, like:
`/shape-product recreate Elemental Battlegrounds faithfully enough to preserve the nostalgia and play feel`

First run:
`bash ./product-shape.sh "$ARGUMENTS"`

Then return a compact product-shaping note with:
- the final product promise in one plain sentence
- the most important fidelity anchors
- the most important edge cases
- what can be simplified in the first milestone
- the smallest proof that would make the direction feel real
- the next command to use

If the user's answers are missing, ask only the few questions needed to fill the artifact.
